MY FAVOURITE ANIME OF ALL TIME

Here is a list of my 10 most favourite animes

Death Note

It is a classic anime, usually anyone who starts anime gets into this with this one. The anime revolves around a young sixteen year old intelligent school boy, Light Yagami, who finds a notebook randomly but the notebook is special because it can kill people!! Light tries to eradicate evil from the world and dreams to become the "GOD OF THE NEW WORLD" but the world's best detective comes in the way. It is best for people who like suspence, thrill and criminial stories.
Rating : 9/10

Demon Slayer

It is another very popular anime known for its tragic stories and top quality animation. The story revoloves around a kind boy named Tanjiro Kamado whose entire family was attacked and killed by the demon king except his sister who is turned to a half demon. The boy seeks revenge and decides to protect other people and along his journey he meets other people, makes friends and fights demon. There is thrill, excitement and a lot of tragic deaths in the anime.
Rating :9/10

Spy x family

This anime revolves around the "Best Spy of Westalis", Twilight, who has to step into the roles of a father and a husband to stop the war, but to complete his mission he adopts a child and accidently arranges a fake marraige with a deadly assain with unhumane abilities. Unaware of his wife's proffesion , he tries to complete his mission.
Rating :8/10
